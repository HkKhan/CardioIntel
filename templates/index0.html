Company Profile

Cardiointel leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to help EMTs in the diagnosis of cardiac conditions in emergencies in order to . The innovation incorporates instrumented helmets (manufactured by other companies) which detect a variety of data points such as impact, velocity of impact and location of impact. Using this data (coupled with historical data), the companies machine learning platform is able to accurately label detailing not only the severity of the impact but the possible short term and long term repercussions.
